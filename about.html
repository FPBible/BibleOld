The FPBible Mission

World English Bible · Fulfilled Perspective

The Fulfilled Prophecy Bible (FPBible) is a guided presentation of Scripture that reads each text within its original historical setting. We take seriously the New Testament’s claim that decisive covenant realities were fulfilled in Christ within the first-century apostolic horizon.

"Everything was announced, accomplished, and revealed in Christ—historical consequences that reached their resolution between AD 30 and AD 70."

Covenantal Reading

Scripture is allowed to speak in its own forensic categories: covenant, temple, kingdom, judgment, and resurrection. Where the New Testament speaks of fulfillment, this project does not postpone that reality. Prophetic imagery is interpreted by Scripture’s own usage and first-century context.

Sustain the Mission

The FPBible is a resource built on hours of forensic research and high-performance AI modeling. To ensure the New Creation reality remains accessible to everyone without paywalls or subscriptions, we rely on the generosity of our readers.
