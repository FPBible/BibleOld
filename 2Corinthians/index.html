Second Letter to the Corinthians

Life in the New Covenant, Glory Without a Veil

2 Corinthians is Paul’s most personal letter. It is written in the aftermath of conflict, misunderstanding, and the collapse of old authority structures.

This letter does not explain how to wait for the New Covenant. It explains how to live because the New Covenant has arrived.
