New Jerusalem / Bride / Zion

New Covenant Identity, Access, and City-Temple Imagery

Framework stub: this page anchors “city / bride / Zion” language to New Creation reality, not future delay.

1) City as covenant identity

Prophets use “city” language as covenant identity (people, worship, access, rule), not merely geography. New Jerusalem is the New Covenant people as a “dwelling with God” reality.

2) Bride imagery

Bride language speaks of covenant union, belonging, and completed marriage imagery—identity and access, not postponement.

3) Zion / mountain language

Zion imagery is covenant-centre language: unshakable kingdom, secure access, priestly nearness. It is meant to locate you in present reality, not keep you waiting.

Core texts (WEB)

  • Revelation 21 (WEB)
  • Revelation 22 (WEB)
  • Hebrews 12 (WEB) — Zion, city, unshakable kingdom
