Colossians

Fulfilled Prophecy · Christ Supreme · New Creation Identity

Colossians is a declaration of completeness. It confronts every form of “Jesus plus” religion and exposes it as regression. Christ is not the beginning of the Christian life — He is the entire reality of it.

This letter announces freedom from:

  • rule-based holiness
  • spiritual intimidation
  • shadow-world identity
  • fear-driven religion
