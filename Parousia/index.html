The Parousia

A modern re-examination of the Parousia as a fulfilled, first-century event — and the present-tense life that flows from it.

This work approaches eschatology through Scripture, covenant context, and historical fulfillment rather than speculation, delay, or fear-based expectation. It argues that the Parousia is not an event still awaited, but a reality already established — reshaping how faith, judgment, hope, and presence are understood today.
