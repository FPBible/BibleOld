Romans

Fulfilled Paraphrase — New Creation Lens

Romans is not a future end-times manual. It is Paul’s covenant-level explanation of the Gospel: the end of condemnation in Adam, the end of Law as covenant authority, and the life of the Spirit as New Creation reality for Jew and Gentile together.
