Colossians

Fulfilled Paraphrase · Christ Supreme · New Creation Identity

Colossians is not a book about “how to become spiritual.” It is a demolition of religious add-ons. Paul declares Christ as the full reality — and exposes anything that tries to compete with Him: ritual, mysticism, fear, law, and self-made holiness.

The message is blunt: You are complete in Christ. Stop living like you still need a system to qualify.
