Revelation

Fulfilled Paraphrase — New Creation Lens

Revelation is apocalyptic covenant prophecy written to first-century churches under pressure. It unveils Jesus’ present reign and the covenant judgment that closed the old world and revealed the New Creation reality. The interpretive lens is established in the Preface and assumed throughout every chapter. An optional Appendix is provided for readers who want symbol/idiom clarification in one place.
