Acts

The Last Days · The Transition Generation · The Gospel to the Nations

Acts is the story of the overlap. The new covenant community emerges while the Temple still stands. The gospel goes to the nations before Jerusalem falls.

This is not the story of the church age in general. It is the documented account of the transition generation—the final decades before the old covenant world ended.
