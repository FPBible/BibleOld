Temple Theme

Judgment on the Old Center, Replacement in Christ

In Scripture the temple is not merely a building — it’s the covenant center: sacrifice, priesthood, purity boundaries, and national identity. John’s Gospel presents Jesus as the replacement of that center: God’s presence relocated from stone and system into the Son, and then into a Spirit-indwelt people.

Key Themes

  • Replacement: Jesus embodies what the temple pointed to.
  • Judgment: the corrupt temple order is exposed and removed.
  • Access: approach to God shifts from location to union.

Where it lands in John

  • John 2 announces the replacement of the temple and signals the approaching covenant crisis.
