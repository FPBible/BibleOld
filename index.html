FP Bible

Fulfilled Paraphrase · Covenant Transition · New Creation

This project reads Scripture in its own historical horizon: audience relevance, covenant language, fulfilled judgment, and New Covenant life in Christ. The goal is clarity — not futurist delay, not religious fear — but finished access, Spirit life, and identity in the Son.
