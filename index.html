Fulfilled Paraphrase · New Creation Lens

A covenant-focused Bible project that reads Scripture within its historical horizon, recognising the completion of the Old Covenant order and the present reality of life in Christ.

Gospels

  • John
    Life, light, judgment, and glory revealed as present realities fulfilled in Christ and embodied in the New Creation.

Letters

  • Romans
    Righteousness apart from the law, union in Christ, and the formation of a new humanity beyond covenant boundaries.
  • Galatians
    The definitive end of law-based identity and the clearest declaration that New Creation alone now counts.
  • Hebrews
    The old sacrificial system rendered obsolete and direct access to God established through Christ.
  • Colossians
    Christ as fullness, exposing the emptiness of shadow-based religion and affirming completeness in Him.
  • Ephesians
    One new humanity formed in Christ, seated life, and reconciliation beyond former divisions.
  • Philippians
    Joy, humility, and stability rooted in Christ’s present reign, written from confinement yet marked by peace.
